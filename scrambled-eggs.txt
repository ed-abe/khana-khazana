Prep :

1) Crack eggs in pan
2) Add 1 spoon of butter
3) Saute the mushrooms and tomatoes.

Do not season it yet.

Start heating the pan

1) Take a spatulla and scramble it gently
2) After 2 mins keep the pan asided. This keeps it from  sticking together
3) Again start scrambling and put the pan on the heating stove.
4) Add cheese to the scrambled eggs.
5) Season it with salt and pepper.
6) Add chives or herbs to the pan.
7) Stir it finally and plate it out or put it on a toasted bread.

Add the sauted mushrooms and tomatoes to the plate.
