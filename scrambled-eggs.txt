Prep :

1) Break eggs and drop in pan
2) Add 1 spoon of butter
3) Saute the mushrooms and tomatoes in a separate pan.

Do not season it yet.

Start heating the pan

1) Scramble the eggs gently with a spatually
2) After 2 mins keep the pan aside to prevent sticking.
3) Keep scrambling and heat the pan again
4) Add cheese to the scrambled eggs.
5) Season it with salt and pepper.
6) Add chives or herbs.
7) Stir it well
8) Serve in a plate with toasted bread

Add the sauted mushrooms and tomatoes to the plate.
